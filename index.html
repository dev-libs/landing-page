About Us

Dev-mad-libs is a web and mobile application sculpted for software engineers. We provide smart, witty, creative and hilarious mad libs and jokes for talented developers such as ourselves. As software developers we are powerful and critical thinkers. We lack a platform where we can show off our witty nature and our sense of humor. well at least we did..

How it works ?

Users will input data or information into our web page. Our system will print to the screen jokes and mad-libs to compensate the user's input. The system complies an enhancing and hilarious story with a developer's theme. Here at mad dev libs we specialize in solving bugs for our clients and users. You can have your bug granted a resolution by going to the bugs.html and inputing your information.

Features

Users are able to add edit and remove libs that are connected to their account. Not only can you read our customized libs but the users are able to complete libs and read the final result. Users can post the completed mad libs to a social network of their choice. Libs are separated and categorized for convenience. We are also working on giving the user template mad libs, Themed libs and language categories.
